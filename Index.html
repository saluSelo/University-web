About Our College

Who Are We

The Department of Information Engineering was established at the beginning of the 1997-1998 academic year and joined the Al-Khwarizmi College of Engineering
in the 2002-2003 academic year. The study period is four years, the students are taught various topics in information engineering. These topics cover
a variety oftopics in communications and information engineering, such as software for computer networks and how they can be linked through communications through its security channels. After successfully passing the course subjects, the students were awarded a Bachelor's degree in Information Engineering.
Our Vision And Goals
The Vision The Department of Information and Communications Engineering seeks to graduate students who are able to efficiently address engineering problems
in their field by providing them with modern theoretical and practical curricula related to information and communication engineering.
Our Goal is Develop and improve the teaching capabilities and research capabilities of faculty members so that they can graduate an engineer capable of meeting local and global industrial demands
